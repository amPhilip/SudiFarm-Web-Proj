Introduction

Sudi Farm is the leading Agriculture Firm. Specialized Farming Techniques, good food and Productive Agriculture. With Dairy firms, associations, scientific organizations, and other partners who are members of the Sudi Farm Platform work together in a pre-competitive manner to produce evidence about the importance of dairy in diets and to demonstrate the sector's commitment to ethical food production.

In order to promote the proper consumption of nutrient-rich dairy goods and highlight the sector's contribution to sustainable agriculture, GDP has been leading the collaborative efforts of the dairy industry for more than ten years.

Together, we want to show how dairy helps people maintain healthy diets and sustainable lifestyles and contribute to the world's food systems.

Sudi Farm Vision

In its 60 years of operation, Sudi Farm's function has continued to change in accordance with shifting government priorities and regulations, driving industrial development. Food, employment, wages, and wealth are all key components of the dairy and agricultural industries.

More About Sudi Farm

By combining customer demand, Sudi Farm is creating stronger, more effective supply chains that promote supplier business growth, retailer quality consistency, and most crucially, increased food security for all Africans.
